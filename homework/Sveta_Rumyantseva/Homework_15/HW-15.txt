Task2:
Go to first line in file:  less <path/file_name>
g or < or ESC-<

Go to last line in file:  less <path/file_name>
G or > or ESC->

Forward one window:  less <path/file_name>
SPACE or Ctrl+V or f or Ctrl+F 

Forward one line:  less <path/file_name>
Enter or Return or e or Ctrl+E or Ctrl+N  or j or Ctrl+J

Backward one line:  less <path/file_name>
y or Ctrl+Y or Ctrl+P or k or Ctrl+K

Search forward for matching line: less <path/file_name>
/pattern

Search backward for matching line:  less <path/file_name>
?pattern

Repeat previous search:  less <path/file_name>
n

Repeat previous search in reverse direction:  less <path/file_name>
N
